Understanding DMARC, SPF & DKIM

What is DMARC?

DMARC is an email security protocol that helps protect your domain from email spoofing and phishing attacks. It builds on SPF and DKIM.

What is SPF?

SPF allows you to specify which mail servers are authorized to send email for your domain.

What is DKIM?

DKIM adds a digital signature to your emails to verify they haven't been tampered with during transit.

Reading Your Dashboard

Pass Rate: Percentage of emails passing BOTH SPF and DKIM. Fail Rate: Emails failing BOTH checks. Disposition: None (delivered), Quarantine (spam), Reject (blocked).
